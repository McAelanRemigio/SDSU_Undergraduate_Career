## MATH 252 – Calculus III (Multivariable Calculus)

### Core Topics Learned
- Vector algebra and geometry, including dot and cross products
- Vector-valued functions and space curves
- Functions of multiple variables and partial derivatives
- Tangent planes, linearization, and gradient-based reasoning
- Optimization with constraints using Lagrange multipliers
- Double and triple integrals in multiple coordinate systems
- Vector fields, line integrals, and major integral theorems
  (Green’s, Stokes’, and Divergence Theorems)

### Mathematical Skills Developed
- Geometric interpretation of multivariable derivatives
- Translating between algebraic, graphical, and physical viewpoints
- Solving optimization problems under constraints
- Applying Jacobians when changing variables in integrals
